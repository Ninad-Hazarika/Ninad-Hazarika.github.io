Smart Plant Monitoring & Automated Irrigation System

Apr – Jun 2026
Tezpur University coursework build

An ESP32-based IoT rig that waters and watches a plant so you don't have to. Soil moisture, temperature/humidity, water level, IR, and touch sensors feed a relay-controlled pump and an animated OLED status readout.

  • Automated irrigation triggered by live soil-moisture thresholds
  • Wi-Fi dashboard for remote monitoring and manual pump override
  • Telegram alerts for low water, prolonged dryness, and motion detected
